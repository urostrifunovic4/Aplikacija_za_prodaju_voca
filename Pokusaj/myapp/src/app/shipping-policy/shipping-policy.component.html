Shipping Services

We provide the below shipping services. Actual delivery time frames may vary due to weather, service delays, holidays etc.

Free standard shipping is only available on select orders. Delivery outside of Serbia may take longer than expected due to numerous procedures and location.

Standard Shipping (6-10 business days)

  • Standard shipping is our least expensive option and has an average delivery window of 3-10 days depending on the final delivery location zip code. Tracking is available.
  • Final delivery is made by Post of Serbia.
  • Select Standard shipping for orders being shipped to a P.O. Box.

Express Shipping (2 Business Days)

  • Express shipping is an expedited option that offers full door-to-door tracking.
  • Express shipping is an available shipping option throughout only Serbia and Europe.
  • Express shipping will arrive by 7:00PM.
  • This service, maybe, is not available for individual countries.

Overnight (Next Business Day)

  • Overnight is an expedited shipping option that offers full door-to-door tracking.
  • Overnight is an available shipping option throughout only Serbia and Europe.
  • Overnight will arrive the next business day by 10:30 a.m. to most Serbia addresses; by noon, 4:30 p.m. or 5 p.m. in remote areas; by noon, 1:30 p.m. or 4:30 p.m. on Saturdays.
  • This service, maybe, is not available for individual countries.
